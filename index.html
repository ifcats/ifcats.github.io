Integrative Family Care at the Springs

Treating the whole person: body, mind, and spirit.
Who We Are

We are a small private Integrative Family practice. In the Springs wing of Clifton Springs Hospital and Clinic, we work closely with practices of massage, chiropractic, acupuncture, balneology and naturopathy.

Our Approach

It is our belief that practices that have survived the centuries have much to offer with conventional medical care. Our focus, not on disease, but on health and wellness, integrates the best that all of these practices have to offer.

What We Do

We work with our patients so they have more control of the healthcare they receive. Diet, exercise, environmental modifications, and even pharmaceutical drugs are used to optimize health and minimize disease.
