Providers

Mary Coan M.D., Ph.D.
M.D., University of Rochester, 1997
Ph.D., Toxicology, University of Rochester, 1982

Dr. Coan is Board Certified in Family Medicine, and in Integrative and Holistic Medicine. Her experience working in both the Chemical and Pharmaceutical Industries, has significantly influenced her practice of medicine. Not confined to the conventional medical paradigm, she believes that the best medicine is an integration of the best that all practices have to offer.
