What is Patient Portal?

The Patient Portal is one of the many requirements mandated by the government in the move to electronic medical records (EMR). We use eClinicalWorks as our EMR vendor, and, as part of their service, they provide a patient portal, housed on their servers, which allows patients to access their own personal health information online. For more information, please visit http://eclinicalworks.com/products-patient-portal.htm .

Logging in to Patient Portal

To access your patient portal, please give the receptionist your personal email address. Once your email address has been entered, eClinicalWorks will contact you, by email, with your login information.
